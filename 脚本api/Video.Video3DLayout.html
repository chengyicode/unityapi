Video3DLayout

enumeration

Description 描述

Types of 3D content layout within a video.

Properties 属性

No3DVideo does not have any 3D content.
SideBySide3DVideo contains 3D content where the left eye occupies the left half and right eye occupies the right half of video frames.
OverUnder3DVideo contains 3D content where the left eye occupies the upper half and right eye occupies the lower half of video frames.
