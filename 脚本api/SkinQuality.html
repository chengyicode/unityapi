SkinQuality

enumeration

Description 描述

The maximum number of bones affecting a single vertex.

See Also: SkinnedMeshRenderer.quality.

Properties 属性

AutoChooses the number of bones from the number current QualitySettings. (Default)
Bone1Use only 1 bone to deform a single vertex. (The most important bone will be used).
Bone2Use 2 bones to deform a single vertex. (The most important bones will be used).
Bone4Use 4 bones to deform a single vertex.
