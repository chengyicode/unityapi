ILightingExplorerExtension

interface in UnityEditor

Description 描述

Interface for when you extend the Lighting Explorer. Used in full overrides of the default behavior.

Public Methods 公共方法

GetContentTabsReturns the tabs that you have selected to display in the Lighting Explorer.
OnDisableThis is called when the Lighting Explorer OnDisable is called, or when you switch to another extension.
OnEnableThis is called when the Lighting Explorer OnEnable is called, or when you switch to another extension.
