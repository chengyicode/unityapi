InteractionSourceState.touchpadPosition

public Vector2 touchpadPosition;

Description 描述

Normalized coordinates for the position of a touchpad interaction.

(0, 0) is the resting position of the touchpad, or if the touchpad isn't touched at the time. Each component lies in the range [-1, 1]. -1 maps to left on the x-axis and down on the y-axis; 1 maps to right on the x-axis and up on the y-axis.
